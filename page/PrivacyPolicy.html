Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 26, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome! This Privacy Policy applies to the Rotana.dev website, all Chrome Extensions, and mobile applications developed by Kong Rotana (hereafter referred to as "we," "us," or "our"). This includes but is not limited to:

  • Website: Rotana.dev
  • Chrome Extensions: Rotana Password Generator, and future extensions
  • Mobile Applications: CV Builder Pro (Android) and any future mobile apps we may release

We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy. Our fundamental principle is simple: we prioritize your privacy and only collect minimal data necessary for app functionality. Most operations happen locally on your device.

2. General Principle: Minimal Data Collection

Across our website, Chrome Extensions, and mobile applications, we adhere to a minimal data collection policy:

  • We do not collect personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, or precise location) unless explicitly required for app functionality.
  • We do not track your browsing activity or collect detailed analytics about your usage patterns.
  • All user-generated content (such as CV data, or passwords) is processed and stored locally on your device by default.

3. How Our Applications Work

3.1 Chrome Extensions

All our Chrome Extensions are built to operate entirely on your device:

  • Local Processing: Any data you provide is processed locally by JavaScript in your browser. No information is sent to our servers.
  • Clipboard Usage: When you use "copy to clipboard" features, we use your browser's native functionality without tracking what you copy.
  • Permissions: We request minimal permissions required for functionality (e.g., activeTab) and use them solely for stated purposes.

3.2 CV Builder Pro Mobile App

Our CV Builder Pro app is designed with privacy in mind:

  • Local Data Storage: All CV data, templates, and user information are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your CV content or personal information.
  • No Account Required: You can use the app without creating an account or providing personal information.
  • Advertising: The app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data according to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Crash Reporting: We may collect anonymous crash reports and basic app performance data to improve stability. This data does not include any personal information or CV content.
  • Export Function: When you export your CV as PDF, the file is generated locally on your device and saved to your chosen location.

4. Data We May Collect

4.1 CV Builder Pro App

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version.
  • Ad-Related Data: Google AdMob collects advertising ID, device identifiers, and usage patterns.
  • Crash Data: Anonymous technical information about app crashes.
  • We do NOT collect: Your CV content, personal information entered in the app, contact details, photos, or any user-generated content.

4.2 Website & Chrome Extensions

We do not collect any personal data from our website or Chrome Extensions.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: Used in CV Builder Pro app. AdMob collects data according to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • No other analytics or trackers: We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or other tracking services in our website or Chrome Extensions.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All user-created content in CV Builder Pro is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by the Flutter framework.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not upload your data to cloud servers or maintain any backups of your CV content.
  • Device Security: The security of your data depends on your device's security measures. We recommend using device encryption and screen locks.

7. Your Rights and Control

  • Data Access: All your data is stored locally on your device and accessible only to you.
  • Data Deletion: You can delete your CV data at any time by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.
  • Ad Personalization: You can control ad personalization through your device settings or Google Ad Settings.
  • Opt-Out: You can reset your advertising ID in your device settings to limit ad tracking.

8. Children's Privacy

Our applications are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Cookies

The Rotana.dev website and our Chrome Extensions do not use cookies or any other tracking technologies. CV Builder Pro app does not use cookies but may store preferences locally on your device.

10. International Data Transfer

Since all user data is stored locally on your device, there is no international data transfer. Third-party services like Google AdMob may transfer data internationally according to their policies.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, new features, or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

12. Legal Compliance

We comply with applicable data protection laws including GDPR (for European users) and other regional privacy regulations. Our minimal data collection approach ensures compliance with most privacy frameworks.
